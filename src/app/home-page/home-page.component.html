Ultimate Rock Paper Scissors

This is a variation on the hit game "Rock, Paper, Scissors" that allows you to throw 7 different actions
Test your skills against another opponent and prove your worth in this new, more challenging version of Rock Paper Scissors!
The Battle Ground page takes you to the main game. The Results page shows you all past matches played with your opponent and the winners of those bouts
There is a control for the background music on the bottom right of the Battle Ground page if you so wish to listen
